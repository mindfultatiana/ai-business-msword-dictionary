## [1.0.0] — 2026-04-19

### Added
- Initial release of `AI_biz_dictionary.dic` with 600+ terms across four domains:
  - **Standard Business English** — compound verbs, project/meeting vocabulary, directional modifiers (e.g., `operationalize`, `workstream`, `swimlane`, `timebox`, `interdepartmentally`)
  - **Executive / MBA Jargon** — frameworks, financial acronyms, strategy vocabulary (e.g., `EBITDA`, `MECE`, `RACI`, `VUCA`, `blitzscaling`, `blue-ocean`)
  - **Startup / SaaS / AI** — GTM motion, architecture patterns, AI/ML terminology (e.g., `ARR`, `churn`, `RAG`, `RLHF`, `fine-tuned`, `guardrails`, `microservices`, `serverless`)
  - **HR / People Ops** — DEI language, talent lifecycle, culture vocabulary (e.g., `allyship`, `neurodivergent`, `reskilling`, `offboarding`, `culture-add`, `EVP`)
- `README.md` with installation instructions for Word on Windows and Mac, contribution guidelines, and domain roadmap
- `CHANGELOG.md` (this file)
- MIT `LICENSE`
